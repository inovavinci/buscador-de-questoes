# Quality Criteria: Vestibular Questions

## Accuracy (Weight: 40%)
- The enunciado must be identical to the original source.
- All alternatives (A-E) must be present and correct.
- The answer key must be confirmed and matched to the correct question.

## Completeness (Weight: 30%)
- Images belonging to the question must be present or marked correctly.
- Metadata (Banca, Year) must be present.
- Difficulty classification must be justified by content analysis.

## Formatting (Weight: 20%)
- Professional look suitable for teacher materials.
- Consistent font sizes and spacing.

## Relevance (Weight: 10%)
- Questions must be pertinent to the requested topic and board.
